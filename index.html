About Me

Hi and welcome! I’m Julia Shin, and this little website is my internet portfolio. I am a fashion designer, blogger, social media content creator, yelp elite food critic, micro-influencer, as well as a freelance creative marketing director, and finally a learning full stack developer.

I love to delivering dynamic content with an artful eye and substance. Graduated with a degree in Fashion Design at FIDM (Fashion Institue of Design and Merchandising) in Los Angeles, CA. A not so ordinary girl with nothing more than passion, a laptop and a dream. I’m proud to be creating conscious digital programs that in the future might even be able to reach millions. For as long as I can remember, I’ve been insatiably curious about creative potential. I believe that no matter what your dreams or obstacles, you have the power to change your life and, by doing so, you’ll change the world.

P.S. Also passionate about a multitude of seemingly unconnected things: photography, hip-hop, stocks, psychology, entrepreneurship, creativity, fitness, just to name a few.
